Introductions

Born in Hong Kong, I journeyed to the UK, fueled by my aspirations to join the games industry. I furthered these ambitions by earning a BSc (Hons) in Computer Games Development from Staffordshire University, where I proudly secured a 2:1 grade.

I have a diverse array of hobbies that I'm passionate about. Firstly, I'm an avid gamer, and some of my all-time favorites include such as Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, the Monster Hunter series, Pokemon, and Final Fantasy XIV. In addition, I have a deep appreciation for game soundtracks, often finding inspiration in them for my own projects where I compose soundtracks. On a more traditional note, I practice Iaido, a Japanese martial art centered on the swift drawing of the katana. My creative side is also evident in my enjoyment of assembling plastic models, particularly those from the Gundam franchise, commonly referred to as Gunpla. And, delving into the realm of fantasy, I cherish playing Dungeons and Dragons, relishing both the roles of a player and a dungeon master.

I am fluent in both English and Chinese, encompassing proficiency in Cantonese and Mandarin. Furthermore, driven by my fascination with Japan, I've embarked on self-study of Japanese, enabling me to read and converse in the language to a certain degree.
